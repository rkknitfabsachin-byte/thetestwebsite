Crafting Quality Since 1997

Established in 1997, RK Knit Fab was founded on a singular vision: to manufacture knitted fabrics that set the benchmark for consistency and performance in real-world garment production.

What began as a specialized boutique operation has evolved into a powerhouse supply partner. We direct all our activities toward exceeding the expectations of our global clientele, maintaining a culture of absolute transparency and moral business integrity.

Under the visionary leadership of CEO Kartik Bhalla, we have attained exponential growth, continuously refining our processes to deliver fabrics that our customers can trust, reorder, and build their legacies upon.
